> At Argo Media Group, we provide high end video production services for commercial, documentary, music video, short and long form narratives, and everything in between. **Services section intro:**
> We get excited just talking about new projects, and we genuinely love working through every step of the process!
>
> It is our passion for the art and craft of video and storytelling that drives us to exceed your expectations, and aim for a higher mark with every job we take on.
>
> We are enthusiastic partners through every stage of production. A lot goes into producing great video content. We make it easy to follow, and we strive for excellence in every part of the process.

**Pre-production / Writing:**
> Every great video starts with an idea. We can work from an existing script, or work with you to develop the project bringing our own writing expertise. Working from a solid starting point is essential. The pre-production phase works out all the logistics of a project (locations, crew, equipment, etc.)

**Production / Filming:**
> Shooting days are tons of fun! This is also the most costly part of the process, with the most people involved, crew, equipment, and so on. This is where all the planning ahead helps us to work efficiently, and maximize production value.

**Post-production / Editing:**
> Everything comes together in the edit. Here's where we carefully craft the final video with high quality music, titles, animation, cinematic color grading, a little creative secret sauce, et voila! The most satisfying part of our job? Blowing people away with the finished product.

**Location:**
> Based in the Denver area in beautiful Colorado. While we love calling the Rocky Mountains home (it's pretty incredible here), we are suckers for travel and going to new places! We have done work all across the U.S. and we'll go pretty much any place you can lug a camera.
